# 🛍️ Retail Sales Performance Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Retail Sales Performance Dashboard** is an interactive and insight-driven business intelligence solution developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. This project analyzes multi-year retail sales data (2019–2024) to track key performance indicators, identify sales trends, and evaluate profitability across products, regions, states, and customer segments.

The dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders monitor performance, compare year-over-year sales, and make data-driven strategic decisions.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Track overall sales, quantity, profit, and profit margin
* Analyze product-level performance and top-selling items
* Compare sales across store types (Online, Offline, Franchise)
* Identify regional and state-wise performance trends
* Monitor monthly sales vs previous year (PY) comparison
* Evaluate top-performing cities by sales, quantity, and profit
* Enable dynamic filtering by Year and Customer Segment

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales:** 4.72bn
* **Total Quantity:** 316K
* **Total Profit:** 873.55M
* **Profit Margin:** 18.49%

The KPI cards include trend indicators to quickly visualize performance movement over time.

## 📈 Dashboard Features & Visualizations

### 1️⃣ Top 10 Sales by Product

* Highlights highest revenue-generating products
* Helps identify strong-performing and underperforming product categories

### 2️⃣ Sales % by Store Type

* Donut chart showing contribution from:

  * Online
  * Offline
  * Franchise
* Useful for channel performance analysis

### 3️⃣ Sales % by Region

* Regional distribution (North, South, East, West)
* Supports geographical strategy planning

### 4️⃣ Total Sales by State

* Interactive map visualization
* Displays state-wise performance insights
* Helps identify high-growth and low-performing states

### 5️⃣ Total Sales vs Previous Year (Monthly Comparison)

* Month-wise comparison of current sales vs PY sales
* Enables trend and seasonal performance analysis

### 6️⃣ Top 5 Cities by Sales | Quantity | Profit

* Tabular visual with key metrics
* Highlights most profitable cities
